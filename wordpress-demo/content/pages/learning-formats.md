# Learning Formats

Demo University offers flexible learning formats to meet different schedules and preferences. Choose the option that best fits your lifestyle and learning goals.

## Available formats

- Online learning for students who prefer remote access and flexible schedules
- Campus learning for students who want in-person classes and on-campus resources
- Hybrid options that blend online and on-campus experiences, when available

## Format support

Each format includes access to advising, academic resources, and faculty support. If you are deciding between online and campus study, we can help you compare what works best for your goals.
