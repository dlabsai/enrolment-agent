# Student Life

Student life at Demo University is built around connection, support, and community. Whether you learn online or on campus, you can access resources designed to help you thrive.

## Community and support

- Student clubs and interest groups
- Academic support and tutoring
- Wellness resources and personal support
- Events, workshops, and networking opportunities

## Stay connected

You will have opportunities to build relationships with peers and faculty throughout your program. Advisors can help you connect with services that fit your goals and learning format.
