# Admissions

Admissions at Demo University focuses on helping you find the right program and learning format. Our advisors provide personalized guidance from your first question through enrollment.

## What admissions can help with

- Program selection and fit
- Learning format options
- Transcript evaluation and next steps
- Connecting you with the right support services

## A supportive process

Our approach is consultative. We listen to your goals, answer your questions, and help you identify the right academic path. If you are ready to apply, you can start the application and connect with an admissions advisor for personalized guidance.
