# Artificial Intelligence, BS

The Artificial Intelligence, BS program introduces the foundations of intelligent systems, data-driven modeling, and responsible AI practices. You will learn how AI concepts are applied in real-world contexts.

## Program overview

This program blends computing fundamentals with applied AI topics. You will explore how data is used to build models and how to evaluate results responsibly.

## What you will study

- Foundations of AI and machine learning
- Data modeling and evaluation basics
- Intelligent systems concepts
- Responsible and ethical AI practices

## Learning outcomes

- Apply foundational AI concepts to practical problems
- Interpret model outputs and evaluate results
- Communicate AI concepts clearly to diverse audiences
