# Digital Marketing, MS

The Digital Marketing, MS program advances strategic, analytical, and creative capabilities for marketing leaders. You will learn to design data-informed campaigns and evaluate performance across channels.

## Program overview

This program focuses on advanced strategy, analytics, and audience insights. You will explore how to align marketing goals with organizational objectives and measure outcomes effectively.

## What you will study

- Advanced audience analytics and segmentation
- Integrated campaign strategy
- Marketing performance measurement
- Responsible communication and ethics

## Learning outcomes

- Design multi-channel marketing strategies grounded in data
- Evaluate performance and optimize marketing outcomes
- Communicate strategy with clarity and purpose

Online and campus options may be available. Ask an admissions advisor to confirm the best format for your goals.
