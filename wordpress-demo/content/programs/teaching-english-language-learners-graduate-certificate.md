# Teaching English Language Learners Graduate Certificate

This graduate certificate prepares educators and support professionals to design inclusive learning experiences for multilingual students. The curriculum emphasizes language development, culturally responsive practice, and evidence-based instruction.

## Program overview

You will explore how language learning intersects with culture, identity, and classroom practice. Coursework focuses on strategies that support student engagement and language growth across subject areas.

## What you will study

- Language acquisition and literacy development
- Instructional strategies for multilingual learners
- Assessment and progress monitoring
- Family and community engagement

## Learning outcomes

- Create lesson plans that support language growth and content mastery
- Use data to adapt instruction for diverse learner needs
- Apply culturally responsive and inclusive teaching practices
