# Graphic Design, BFA

The Graphic Design, BFA program builds skills in visual communication, typography, and design thinking. Students develop a strong portfolio through project-based learning.

## Program overview

You will explore how visual systems communicate ideas and how design choices shape user experience. Projects emphasize clarity, consistency, and intentional storytelling across different media.

## What you will study

- Visual design principles and typography
- Branding and identity systems
- Design research and critique
- Digital and print production workflows

## Learning outcomes

- Create cohesive visual systems across media
- Apply design critique to improve creative outcomes
- Present and defend design decisions
