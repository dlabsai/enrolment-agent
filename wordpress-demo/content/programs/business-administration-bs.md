# Business Administration, BS

The Business Administration, BS program builds a foundation in management, operations, and strategic decision-making. You will explore how organizations plan, lead, and grow in a changing business landscape.

## Program overview

This program emphasizes practical application, ethical leadership, and analytical thinking. Coursework introduces core business functions and how they work together to achieve organizational goals.

## What you will study

- Management and organizational behavior
- Marketing and customer strategy
- Financial and managerial decision-making
- Operations and process improvement

## Learning outcomes

- Analyze business challenges and propose strategic solutions
- Apply foundational management and marketing concepts
- Communicate business ideas clearly to diverse audiences

Online and campus options may be available. Ask an admissions advisor to confirm the best format for your goals.
