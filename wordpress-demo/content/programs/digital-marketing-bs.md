# Digital Marketing, BS

The Digital Marketing, BS program combines strategy, analytics, and creative communication. You will learn how to plan, execute, and evaluate campaigns across digital channels.

## Program overview

You will study how audiences engage with content, how data informs decisions, and how marketing goals connect to organizational strategy. The curriculum balances analytical thinking with creative problem-solving.

## What you will study

- Audience research and segmentation
- Content strategy and storytelling
- Marketing analytics and performance measurement
- Ethics and responsible communication

## Learning outcomes

- Build data-informed marketing strategies
- Evaluate campaign performance using analytics
- Communicate brand messages with clarity and purpose

Online and campus options may be available. Ask an admissions advisor to confirm the best format for your goals.
