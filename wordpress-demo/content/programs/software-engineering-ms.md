# Software Engineering, MS

The Software Engineering, MS program emphasizes scalable systems, software architecture, and collaborative development practices. You will deepen your technical expertise and leadership in modern software projects.

## Program overview

You will study how to design, build, and maintain complex software systems with a focus on reliability and quality. The curriculum highlights modern engineering practices and team-based delivery.

## What you will study

- Software architecture and design patterns
- Testing, quality, and reliability practices
- Cloud and distributed systems concepts
- Team collaboration and project delivery

## Learning outcomes

- Design robust software systems at scale
- Apply engineering practices to improve quality and maintainability
- Lead technical projects with clear communication

Online and campus options may be available. Ask an admissions advisor to confirm the best format for your goals.
