# Public Health, MPH

The Public Health, MPH program focuses on population health, program design, and data-driven decision-making. You will explore how communities and systems influence health outcomes.

## Program overview

The curriculum emphasizes evidence-based practice, ethical decision-making, and communication. You will learn how to interpret data and design interventions that address complex community needs.

## What you will study

- Epidemiology and biostatistics foundations
- Health policy and program planning
- Community health assessment
- Program evaluation and improvement

## Learning outcomes

- Use data to understand health trends and needs
- Design and evaluate public health interventions
- Communicate health information clearly and responsibly
