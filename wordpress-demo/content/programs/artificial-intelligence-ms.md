# Artificial Intelligence, MS

The Artificial Intelligence, MS program focuses on intelligent systems, data-driven decision-making, and applied machine learning. You will study how AI technologies are built, evaluated, and applied responsibly.

## Program overview

This program blends theory with practical implementation. You will explore how models learn from data, how to evaluate results, and how to apply AI techniques to real-world problems.

## What you will study

- Machine learning foundations
- Data modeling and evaluation
- Natural language and intelligent systems concepts
- Responsible and ethical AI practices

## Learning outcomes

- Build and evaluate AI models using data-driven approaches
- Apply AI techniques to solve practical problems
- Communicate AI results clearly to technical and non-technical audiences
