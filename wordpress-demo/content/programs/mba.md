# Business Administration, MBA

The Business Administration, MBA program focuses on leadership, strategy, and organizational decision-making. You will explore how to guide teams, evaluate business challenges, and make data-informed decisions.

## Program overview

This program is designed for professionals who want to strengthen leadership and management skills. Coursework integrates strategic thinking, operational planning, and ethical decision-making.

## What you will study

- Leadership and organizational strategy
- Financial and managerial decision-making
- Operations and process improvement
- Business analytics and performance measurement

## Learning outcomes

- Evaluate business scenarios and propose strategic solutions
- Apply leadership principles to complex organizational contexts
- Communicate decisions effectively to stakeholders

## Format options

Online and campus options may be available. Ask an admissions advisor to confirm the best format for your goals.
