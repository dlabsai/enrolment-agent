# Public Health, BS

The Public Health, BS program introduces the foundations of population health, prevention, and community well-being. You will learn how social, environmental, and organizational factors influence health outcomes.

## Program overview

Coursework emphasizes data-informed thinking, community engagement, and ethical practice. You will explore how public health initiatives are designed, implemented, and evaluated.

## What you will study

- Foundations of public health practice
- Community health and prevention
- Health systems and policy basics
- Data and program evaluation concepts

## Learning outcomes

- Describe key factors that influence population health
- Apply public health concepts to community contexts
- Communicate health information responsibly
