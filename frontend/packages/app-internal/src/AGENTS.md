## Open the Internal App

- Open `http://localhost:5174/`
- Login `admin@example.com` (ask user for password)

## Navigation

Use the sidebar links or hash URLs:

- **Chat**: click the Chat icon in the sidebar or navigate to `#/chat`
- **Chats**: click the Chats icon in the sidebar or navigate to `#/chats`
- **Usage**: click the Usage icon in the sidebar or navigate to `#/usage`
- **Traces**: click the Traces icon in the sidebar or navigate to `#/traces`
- **Chat Analytics**: click the Chat Analytics icon in the sidebar or navigate to `#/analytics`
- **Public Analytics**: click the Public Analytics icon in the sidebar or navigate to `#/public-analytics`
- **Evals**: click the Evals icon in the sidebar or navigate to `#/evals`
- **Instructions**: click the Instructions icon in the sidebar or navigate to `#/instructions`
- **Settings**: click the Settings icon in the sidebar or navigate to `#/settings`
